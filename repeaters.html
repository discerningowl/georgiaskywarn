Georgia SKYWARN Linked Repeater System

Many of these repeaters are part of the Southeastern Linked Repeater Net, which meets every Wednesday at 9 pm Eastern. It's one of the largest RF-linked nets in the southeast and has been on the air since 1993.

Local SKYWARN Nets (Not-Linked)

These repeaters are activated by County SKYWARN coordinators or County EMA. These are not-linked to the state-wide repeater network. Local officials will pass weather reports on to WX4PTC or the NWS.
